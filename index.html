What GeoLocale does

Moving to a new city, or just a new part of town? GeoLocale helps you figure out where you actually want to live before you sign anything.

Pick any city and get to know it the way locals do, one neighborhood at a time. Tell GeoLocale what your life looks like, and it ranks neighborhoods around your priorities. Every score is built from real places on the map: schools, transit stops, parks, clinics, cafes, gyms and shops, so you can see at a glance what is actually nearby. In supported cities you can go further and browse real property listings right on the map.

Private by design

GeoLocale has no accounts and no sign-in. It does not track you, does not access your device location, and shows no ads. Your preferences stay on your device. Read the full privacy policy.
